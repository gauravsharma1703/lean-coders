STUDENT LOAN CRITERIA

  • The candidate applying for the loan must be a resident of India.
  • He/she must have confirmed admission in recognised educational institutes in India or abroad.
  • he age of the candidate must fall within the bracket of 18 to 35 years during loan application.
  • He/she must be undergoing a graduate/postgraduate degree or a PG diploma.
  • The applicant should have a secured admission in a college or university affiliated by
    Grants Commission (UGC)/All India Council for
    Technical Education (AICTE)/Govt. etc.
  • The co-applicant must have a regular income source.
